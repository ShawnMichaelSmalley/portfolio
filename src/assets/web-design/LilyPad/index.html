Less than a mile from the beach and 1 mile to Seaside Center...

Under new ownership since May 2017. 4+ Bedroom, 3 Bath with private pool on a canal within walking and biking distance to Seaside Proper and Seagrove Center. The home is 3 blocks to the beach and a 5 min bike ride to Seaside Town Center (Proper). This home has a cottage house attached for the full 4+ bedrooms. To fully utilize the property a mix of adults and children are best. This property can only handle a max of 8 adults due to bed count. Close to the grocery store (Publix), banks, liquor store, dry cleaning and more.

Experience the charm and nature of Old Seagrove Beach in this recently renovated 4+ bedroom, 3 bath cottage that overlooks the canal. Paddle your troubles away to Western Lake in a canoe or kayak – if you’re lucky, nature will provide you with an opening into the Gulf of Mexico! A ten minute walking stroll will take you into Seaside where you can enjoy award-winning restaurants, unique shops and the ambiance of a quaint coastal village. Private lagoon-shaped pool surrounded by flagstone pavers and landscaping with loungers, tables and chairs for your relaxation. Courtyard lights cast a glow while grilling fresh seafood or burgers for dinner. The Lily Pad is perfect for 1-2 families, offering 2 full kitchens and 2 living areas, whole-house Rinnai tank-less water heater system, tongue-in-groove ceilings and both tile and hardwood flooring throughout.

  • First Floor
    The main entrance into The Lily Pad takes you through a courtyard past the laundry room and butler’s pantry, for your convenience in dropping off your beach towels on your way in. The open kitchen, dining and living room all look out over the pool with a gas grill conveniently located on deck just outside the kitchen. Granite countertops and ample cabinetry in both the kitchen and laundry room. Front loading high capacity HE washer and dryer. Kitchen offers all electric GE Profile stainless appliances, including side-by-side fridge, dishwasher, microwave and glass-top range, garbage disposal, over-sink glass cabinetry and lovely pendant lighting. Breakfast bar has 4 bar stools. Step down into the dining area featuring dining table that, with additional leaf, will seat 10 and gas fireplace with custom curved mantle and built-in shelving. Living room has two sofas, 2 side chairs, wall-mounted LCD TV and DVD player. The first bedroom on this floor has twin bunks, (2 twins) LCD TV, separate dressing area with closet, desk and lamp, Twin air matress in the closet and a outside entrance. The second bedroom on this floor has a Queen bed, closet, two side tables with lamps, LCD TV, and bathroom with walk-in shower. French doors from this room also lead into living room.
  • Second Floor
    Lofted master bedroom with King bed, wood floors, ceiling fan, two nightstands with lamps, closet, LCD TV, DVD player and chest of drawers. Private outdoor balcony overlooks the canal and pool, with two chairs, coffee table and loveseat. Large private bath with Jacuzzi tub, granite-topped dual vanities with center make-up table and separate large, tiled walk-in shower.
  • Guest Cottage

    Enter the guest cottage house through the open front porch, up the staircase to the second floor living area is a large loft like area with vaulted ceilings, overhead lights & fans. This area has a full kitchen with Corian counter-tops, glass top range, refrigerator/freezer with ice maker, dishwasher, disposal and microwave. It has a bistro table and 2 chairs plus 3+ bar stools at breakfast bar. Completing the living area is a LCD TV, DVD player, plush side chair with ottoman, coffee and end tables. There is a small closet, sofa is a queen sleeper and there are 2 built-in bunk beds for more sleepability. A full bathroom with walk-in shower is off of the loft area. There is a private queen bedroom with 2 closets, fan and overhead lighting, LCD TV and a chest of drawers.

    Between the king and queen bedrooms on the second floor is a large closet which can be locked off or opened to both or either room. It is a perfect area for a pack & play if needed.
  • Outside
    Seasonal heated lagoon pool with flagstone pavers, lounges, chairs and tables. There is a gas grill on the side deck with overhead patio lighting and parking for 2-3 cars.
  • Payment Info

    Accepted forms of payment: All credit cards, money orders, cashier's checks and personal checks (in advance for clearing), through VRBO. Please ask about our rate specials during certain times. We also have direct pay through our company.
