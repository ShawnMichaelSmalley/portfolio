Mason & Co.
Specializing in custom homes, remodeling, kitchen and bath renovations, and general construction services

Mason & Co. is a premier construction services company that has a combined experience of over 30 years. We specialize in custom homes, remodeling, kitchen and bath renovations, as well as general construction. Whether you plan to transform your kitchen, convert your bathroom into a spa, or add an additional room, Mason and Co. can help you realize your new vision for your home.

Our services are backed by skilled individuals who have vast experience in all areas of their work, and proudly put quality at the forefront of our company. We take the time to construct all projects to perfection and pay particular attention to the details that are important to our customers. This is why the majority of our business is comprised of direct referrals from loyal and satisfied customers.

We also have the ability to work directly with our sister company Interior Gilt, an interior design firm, which enables our customers to have a one-stop shop for all their home renovation needs.

Remodeling your home has never been easier. We have the knowledge and experience to bring your vision to life in a timely and economical manner. No job is too big or too small.

Call Mason and Co. today for a free on-site estimate. We will evaluate your home as it is and listen to your plans. We will give you practical tips in regards to their feasibility, and then prepare a cost estimate for you so you can get started with your project.
