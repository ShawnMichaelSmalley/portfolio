    About us

    Style with a pop of culture

    Opening Fall 2017, S. Powell & Co. helps you make a statement with our elite selection of women's, men's and children's clothing, footwear and accessories. Our goal is to continue the growth of the Uptown 23rd district by bringing a posh take on the typical shopping experience to OKC.

    At S. Powell & Co., you will find a fusion of quality basics and funky statement pieces. The store takes inspiration from key moments in pop culture and its icons. Brands worn by Kate Hudson, Mario Lopez, Sarah Jessica Parker, Rihanna and others are now available locally courtesy of S. Powell & Co. If you strive to set your own trends, we can help! As an early-adopter, S. Powell & Co. also provides exclusive access to pieces that have never before been seen in OKC so your wardrobe will POP.
